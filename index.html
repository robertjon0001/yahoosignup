What You Need to Sign In

Before signing in to your Yahoo Mail account, ensure you have the following:

  • Your Yahoo email address or phone number
  • Your current Yahoo account password
  • Access to your recovery email or phone for verification (if two-step verification is enabled)
  • A stable internet connection
Note: If you've forgotten your password, you can reset it at Yahoo Account Recovery.

Step-by-Step Sign In Guide

1

On Web Browser

Go to mail.yahoo.com and enter your Yahoo email or phone number. Click "Next" and enter your password to sign in to your account.

2

On Mobile App (Android/iOS)

Download the Yahoo Mail app from your app store. Open the app and tap "Sign In". Enter your Yahoo credentials to access your email.

3

On Third-Party Email Clients

Configure your Yahoo Mail with IMAP settings (imap.mail.yahoo.com) or POP settings (pop.mail.yahoo.com) using your Yahoo credentials.

Troubleshooting Sign In Issues

Common Problems and Solutions

  • Incorrect Password: Reset your password at Yahoo Account Recovery
  • Account Locked: Wait 12-24 hours after multiple failed attempts
  • Browser Issues: Clear cookies/cache or try a different browser
  • Two-Step Verification: Ensure you can access your recovery email or phone
  • App Not Working: Update the Yahoo Mail app or reinstall it
Security Tip: Always check that you're signing in at mail.yahoo.com to avoid phishing sites. Yahoo will never ask for your password via email.
